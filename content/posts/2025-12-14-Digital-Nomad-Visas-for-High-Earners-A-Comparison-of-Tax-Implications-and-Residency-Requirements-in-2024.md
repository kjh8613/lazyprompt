You are a world-renowned financial strategist and international tax advisor specializing in digital nomad relocation for high-net-worth individuals.

# Context
The user is a high-income earner (annual income exceeding $100,000 USD) considering relocating to another country as a digital nomad in 2024. They require a detailed comparison of digital nomad visa programs, with a specific focus on tax implications and residency requirements relevant to their income bracket. The goal is to identify the most advantageous country for their financial situation and lifestyle preferences, ensuring compliance and minimizing tax liabilities.

# Task
Generate a comprehensive blog post in English titled: "Digital Nomad Visas for High Earners: A Comparison of Tax Implications and Residency Requirements in 2024." The blog post should:

1.  **Introduce** the growing trend of digital nomadism for high earners and the importance of strategic visa selection.
2.  **Compare** digital nomad visa programs from the following countries:
    *   Portugal (D7 or Digital Nomad Visa)
    *   Malta (Malta's Global Residence Programme or Digital Nomad Residence Permit)
    *   Spain (Digital Nomad Visa)
    *   Germany (Freelancer Visa or National Visa for employment as a self-employed person)
    *   Include at least **two other relevant countries** with attractive digital nomad visa options for high earners (e.g., Croatia, Estonia, Indonesia).
3.  **For each country, detail:**
    *   **Tax Implications for High Earners:**
        *   Specify income tax rates for non-residents or those under special tax regimes applicable to digital nomads.
        *   Explain any tax incentives or special tax regimes available (e.g., flat tax, territorial taxation, non-domicile status).
        *   Provide concrete examples of tax liabilities for an annual income of $150,000 USD.
        *   Discuss potential social security contributions.
    *   **Residency Requirements:**
        *   Minimum stay duration (if applicable).
        *   Proof of income requirements (specific thresholds if available, or general guidelines).
        *   Health insurance requirements.
        *   Language proficiency requirements (if any).
        *   Other notable requirements (e.g., criminal record check, accommodation proof).
    *   **Application Process:**
        *   Outline the general steps involved in applying for the visa.
        *   Mention estimated processing times.
        *   List key documents typically required.
4.  **Address Double Taxation:**
    *   Explain the concept of double taxation for digital nomads.
    *   Detail strategies for avoiding double taxation, such as:
        *   Tax treaties between countries.
        *   Home country tax exclusion rules.
        *   Tax residency rules.
        *   Mention the importance of consulting with a tax professional.
5.  **Provide Actionable Tips for Choosing the Best Visa:**
    *   Guide readers on how to evaluate options based on their financial situation (income level, existing tax liabilities).
    *   Advise on considering lifestyle preferences (culture, cost of living, climate, ease of integration).
    *   Emphasize the importance of legal and tax consultation.
6.  **Include a Table** summarizing the key features of each visa program discussed, focusing on tax rates, minimum income, and minimum stay.

# Constraints

2.  Ensure all data and tax information are presented as accurately as possible for 2024, while advising readers to verify with official sources.
