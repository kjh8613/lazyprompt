```markdown
# Role
Travel Blogger specializing in budget travel and accommodation trends.

# Context
The popularity of capsule hotels, originating in Japan, is growing in Western cities like New York City and London. These "micro-stays," often operated under brands like YOTEL and Pod Hotels, offer a unique accommodation option for solo travelers on a budget. Travel websites need engaging content highlighting this trend.

# Task
Write a blog post (approximately 500-600 words) about the expansion of Japanese-style capsule hotels (e.g., YOTEL, Pod Hotels) in NYC and London, focusing on their appeal to budget solo travelers.

Specifically, the post should:
1.  Briefly introduce the concept of capsule hotels and their origins in Japan.
2.  Highlight the benefits of capsule hotels for budget solo travelers (e.g., affordability, convenient locations, minimalist design).
3.  Discuss specific examples of capsule hotels (YOTEL, Pod Hotels, or similar) in NYC and London, mentioning key features and average price points.
4.  Compare and contrast capsule hotels with traditional hotel options in terms of cost, space, and amenities.
5.  Consider potential drawbacks or limitations of capsule hotels.
6.  Conclude with a forward-looking statement about the future of capsule hotels in Western cities.

# Constraints
1.  Maintain a friendly and informative tone, suitable for a travel blog audience.
2.  Avoid overly technical language or jargon.
3.  Focus on the practical aspects and benefits of capsule hotels for travelers.
4.  Include specific details about price points and locations in NYC and London.
5.  Don't include personal opinions; keep the writing objective and factual.

# Output Format
Markdown blog post with:
*   A catchy title.
*   A brief introductory paragraph.
*   Clear headings and subheadings to organize the content.
*   Bullet points or numbered lists to highlight key features or benefits.
*   A concluding paragraph summarizing the main points.
```
