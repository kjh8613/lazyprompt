### Your Beginner's Guide to Crafting Effective AI Prompts

To unlock the power of AI, you need to speak its language. Here's a structured approach to crafting prompts that deliver, along with ready-to-use templates for common AI tools.

**The Core Components of a High-Performance Prompt:**

1.  **Role:** Assign the AI a specific persona or expertise. This sets the stage for the AI's perspective and knowledge base.
2.  **Context:** Provide background information, the situation, and any relevant details the AI needs to understand the task.
3.  **Task:** Clearly state what you want the AI to do, step-by-step if necessary. Be unambiguous.
4.  **Constraints:** Define limitations, style guides, tone, length, what to avoid, or specific elements to include. These ensure the output meets your quality standards.
5.  **Output Format:** Specify how you want the information presented â€“ a table, bullet points, code block, markdown, JSON, etc.

4.  Prioritize clarity and completeness over brevity.
5.  Do not include any actual project plan timelines; focus on the brief's core elements.

# Output Format
A structured Markdown document with distinct sections for each of the required tasks (Objectives, Scope, Deliverables, Success Metrics, Risks and Assumptions, Resource Requirements). Use bold headings for each section.
