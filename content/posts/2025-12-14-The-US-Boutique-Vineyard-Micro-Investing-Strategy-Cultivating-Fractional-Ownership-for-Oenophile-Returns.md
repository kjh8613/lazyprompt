    *   **Direct Fractional Ownership Platforms:** Detail platforms that facilitate direct stakes in specific vineyards.

7.  **Conclusion:**
    *   Summarize the key takeaways and future outlook for this investment niche.
    *   Reiterate the balance between financial and lifestyle benefits.
