# Context

# Task

    *   When to adjust or pause.
    *   Importance of protein intake and resistance training.
6.  **Target Audience:** Write for individuals who are frustrated with traditional dieting and are seeking a sustainable, science-backed approach.
7.  **Tone:** Empathetic, encouraging, authoritative, and educational. Avoid overly technical jargon where possible, or explain it simply.

2.  Incorporate at least two relatable analogies to simplify complex concepts.
3.  Do not promote specific supplement brands or extreme dietary protocols.
4.  Ensure a logical flow from problem (metabolic damage) to solution (reverse dieting) to practical steps.
5.  Conclude with a strong, motivating call to action for embracing sustainable health practices.
