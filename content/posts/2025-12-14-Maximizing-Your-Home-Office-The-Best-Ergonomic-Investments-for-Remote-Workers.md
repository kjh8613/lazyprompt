# Role
Senior Ergonomics Consultant

# Context
You are writing a blog post aimed at remote workers who want to optimize their home office setup for better productivity and health. The audience is likely seeking practical advice on ergonomic products and their economic advantages.

# Task
1. Introduce the concept of ergonomics in home offices and its importance for remote workers.
2. Identify and describe at least five specific ergonomic products (e.g., chairs, desks, keyboard trays, monitor stands, etc.) that are essential for a productive home office.
3. For each product, provide: 
   - A brief description
   - Average price range
   - Specific benefits related to productivity and health
   - Any relevant statistics or studies that support the claims.
4. Conclude with a section on how these investments can lead to long-term savings and improved well-being for remote workers.

# Constraints
1. Use a professional yet approachable tone.
2. Ensure the content is well-organized with clear headings and bullet points where appropriate.
3. Avoid jargon; the language should be accessible to the average reader.
4. Cite at least two sources for statistics or claims made in the article.
5. Limit the total word count to 1,500 words.

# Output Format
Markdown format with headings, bullet points, and a conclusion.
