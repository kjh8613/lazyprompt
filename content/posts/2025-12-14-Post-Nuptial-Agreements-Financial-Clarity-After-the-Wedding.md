```markdown
# Role
Expert Relationship Law Attorney specializing in marital agreements and asset division.

# Context
Many married couples find themselves facing financial uncertainties and disagreements, especially when a business is started during the marriage or unforeseen circumstances arise. Post-nuptial agreements offer a way to address these issues after the wedding has taken place. The goal is to provide clear information and guidance to couples considering or creating a post-nuptial agreement.

# Task
Write a comprehensive relationship law guide on post-nuptial agreements, covering the following points:

1.  **Introduction:** Begin with a concise introduction explaining what a post-nuptial agreement is.
2.  **Why Create a Post-Nup?**  Explain the common reasons married couples choose to create post-nuptial agreements. Provide at least five distinct reasons.
3.  **Asset Division and Business Ownership:** Dedicate a section to explaining how post-nuptial agreements can be particularly useful in defining asset division, especially when one or both spouses start a business during the marriage.  Provide hypothetical examples of how the agreement might address business valuation, ownership, and future profits.
4.  **Financial Stress Reduction:** Elaborate on how a post-nuptial agreement can alleviate financial stress within a marriage by providing clarity and certainty regarding finances.
5.  **Key Considerations:** Outline key considerations for creating a valid and enforceable post-nuptial agreement. This should include the importance of independent legal counsel, full financial disclosure, and fairness.
6.  **Conclusion:** Summarize the benefits of post-nuptial agreements and encourage couples to seek professional legal advice if considering one.

# Constraints
1.  The guide should be written in clear, concise, and easy-to-understand language, avoiding legal jargon where possible.
2.  Maintain a neutral and objective tone, presenting both the advantages and potential drawbacks of post-nuptial agreements.
3.  The guide should be approximately 800-1200 words in length.
4.  Ensure the information provided is accurate and reflects current legal practices regarding post-nuptial agreements.
5.  Do not provide legal advice specific to any jurisdiction. Instead, provide general guidance applicable across various jurisdictions.

# Output Format
Markdown document.
```
