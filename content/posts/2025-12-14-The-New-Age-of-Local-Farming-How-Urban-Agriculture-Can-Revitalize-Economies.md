4. Provide at least two case studies of successful urban farms, detailing their impact on the local economy.
5. Offer practical tips for readers interested in starting their own urban farms, covering essential steps and considerations.

# Constraints

2. Use subheadings to organize content for better readability.
3. Limit the total word count to 1,200 words.
4. Include at least one quote from an expert in urban agriculture.
5. Cite all case studies and statistics appropriately.

# Output Format
