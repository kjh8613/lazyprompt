    *   Address the sunsetting of the "original" NHR regime and the details of the "NHR 2.0" (if available and relevant).
3.  **Spain's Digital Nomad Visa (and Beckham Law):**
    *   Describe Spain's Digital Nomad Visa requirements (income thresholds, etc.).

    *   Provide a side-by-side comparison of the tax benefits and drawbacks of both Portugal's NHR (and new versions) and Spain's Beckham Law for a high-earning remote worker. Consider factors like income type, length of stay, and future residency plans.
5.  **Important Considerations:**
    *   Highlight the importance of seeking professional tax advice.
    *   Mention other taxes to consider (e.g., VAT, property taxes).
    *   Briefly discuss the potential impact of international tax treaties and double taxation agreements.
6. **Disclaimer:**  Include a clear disclaimer stating that this guide is for informational purposes only and does not constitute professional tax advice. Readers should consult with qualified professionals for personalized guidance.

# Constraints
