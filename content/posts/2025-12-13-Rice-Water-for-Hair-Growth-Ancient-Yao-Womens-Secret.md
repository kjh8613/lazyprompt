Many people are interested in natural and DIY beauty treatments, especially for hair growth. Fermented rice water, used for centuries by the Yao women of China, has gained popularity. A blog post is needed that explains the history, science (specifically inositol), and provides a simple recipe for making fermented rice water at home. The target audience is beauty enthusiasts interested in natural hair care solutions.
