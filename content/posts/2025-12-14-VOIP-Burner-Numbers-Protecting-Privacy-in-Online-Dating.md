# Role
Digital Safety Expert and Tech Writer

# Context
Online dating and classified ad platforms like Craigslist can expose individuals to potential risks, including stalking, harassment, and doxxing. Providing a personal phone number to strangers increases vulnerability. VOIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) apps like Google Voice and Burner offer disposable phone numbers that can be used to protect personal information. Many users are unaware of the benefits of using VOIP services and need a clear, accessible guide on how to use them for enhanced online safety.

# Task
Create a digital safety guide titled "VOIP Burner Numbers: Protecting Privacy in Online Dating" that includes the following sections:

1.  **Introduction:** Briefly explain the risks of sharing personal phone numbers on dating apps and Craigslist. Highlight the benefits of using VOIP burner numbers for privacy.

2.  **Understanding VOIP and Burner Numbers:**
    *   Define VOIP and burner numbers in simple terms.
    *   Explain how they work.
    *   List popular VOIP apps (e.g., Google Voice, Burner, TextFree, Hushed).

3.  **Why Use a Burner Number for Online Dating?**
    *   **Privacy Protection:** Explain how a burner number shields your real number.
    *   **Preventing Stalking and Harassment:** Describe how a disposable number minimizes the risk of unwanted contact.
    *   **Controlling Communication:** Highlight the ability to easily block or dispose of the number if needed.

4.  **Setting Up a VOIP Number:**
    *   Provide step-by-step instructions for setting up a Google Voice number.
    *   Provide step-by-step instructions for setting up a Burner app number.
    *   (Optional) Briefly mention other popular VOIP apps and their setup processes.

5.  **Best Practices for Using Burner Numbers on Dating Apps and Craigslist:**
    *   **When to Share the Number:** Suggest using the burner number *after* establishing some level of trust.
    *   **Initial Screening:** Encourage using the dating app's messaging system first for initial conversations.
    *   **Number Expiration:** Advise setting expiration dates or using temporary numbers when available.
    *   **Monitoring Activity:** Suggest regularly checking the burner number for suspicious activity.
    *   **Deleting the Number:** Explain how and when to safely dispose of the burner number.

6.  **Additional Safety Tips for Online Dating and Craigslist:**
    *   **Meeting in Public Places:** Always meet for the first time in a public, well-lit location.
    *   **Informing a Friend:** Let a friend or family member know where you are going and who you are meeting.
    *   **Trust Your Instincts:** If something feels off, end the date or interaction immediately.
    *   **Reverse Image Search:** Use reverse image search to verify the person's profile pictures.

7.  **Conclusion:** Reiterate the importance of online safety and encourage readers to take proactive steps to protect their privacy.

# Constraints
*   The guide must be written in a clear, concise, and easy-to-understand style.
*   Avoid technical jargon; explain all terms simply.
*   Maintain a tone that is informative and reassuring, emphasizing safety and empowerment.
*   Focus on practical, actionable advice.
*   The guide should be approximately 500-700 words in length.

# Output Format
Markdown document suitable for posting on a blog or sharing as a digital safety resource.
