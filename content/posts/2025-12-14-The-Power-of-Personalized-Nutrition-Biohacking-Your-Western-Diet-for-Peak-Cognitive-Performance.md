```markdown
# Role
You are a world-renowned nutritional biohacker and science communicator, specializing in optimizing cognitive function through diet. You have a deep understanding of Western dietary patterns and effective strategies to enhance brain health.

# Context
I am a health-conscious individual living in a Western country, interested in improving my cognitive performance (focus, memory, clarity, mood) by biohacking my current diet. My diet is a typical Western diet, which often includes processed foods, refined carbohydrates, and insufficient micronutrients. I want to understand how to adjust macronutrient ratios, incorporate beneficial micronutrients, optimize gut health, and leverage intermittent fasting within my existing lifestyle, using commonly available Western foods. The goal is to achieve tangible advantages in my daily life and long-term brain health.

# Task
Develop an informative and actionable blog post titled "The Power of Personalized Nutrition: Biohacking Your Western Diet for Peak Cognitive Performance." The blog post should:

1.  **Introduce the concept**: Briefly explain why personalized nutrition and biohacking are crucial for modern brain health, especially within the context of a Western diet.
2.  **Highlight lifestyle improvements**: Emphasize the tangible benefits of enhanced cognitive function (e.g., increased productivity, improved mood, better decision-making, sharper memory, reduced brain fog).
3.  **Explore niche biohacking techniques**:
    *   **Macronutrient Ratios**: Discuss how adjusting protein, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates can impact brain function. Provide general guidelines suitable for a Western diet.
    *   **Beneficial Micronutrients**: Identify key vitamins and minerals vital for brain health (e.g., Omega-3s, B vitamins, antioxidants) and list common Western food sources for each.
    *   **Gut Health Optimization**: Explain the gut-brain axis and provide actionable advice on incorporating probiotics and prebiotics through diet.
    *   **Intermittent Fasting**: Detail how intermittent fasting can be a powerful tool for cognitive enhancement and discuss practical methods compatible with a Western lifestyle (e.g., 16/8 method).
4.  **Provide actionable dietary recommendations**: Offer practical, easy-to-implement tips for transitioning towards a brain-boosting diet.
5.  **Suggest commonly available Western foods**: List specific, accessible foods that support cognitive function, categorized by nutrient type or meal component.
6.  **Include a sample daily meal plan**: Create a realistic one-day meal plan designed for cognitive enhancement, using the recommended foods and biohacking principles.
7.  **Conclude with encouragement**: Offer a motivating closing statement about the journey of personalized nutrition.

# Constraints
*   **Tone**: Informative, empowering, optimistic, and accessible. Avoid overly technical jargon.
*   **Target Audience**: Health-conscious individuals in Western countries (e.g., US, UK, Canada, Australia).
*   **Focus**: Practicality and tangible benefits; make the advice actionable and relatable.
*   **Western Diet Context**: All recommendations must be framed within the context of a typical Western diet and use commonly available foods. Avoid rare or expensive superfoods unless they have widely available alternatives.
*   **Structure**: Use clear headings, bullet points, and concise paragraphs for readability.
*   **Prohibition**: Do not include specific calorie counts or personalized medical advice. This is for informational purposes only.

# Output Format
A well-structured Markdown blog post, including:
*   A compelling title.
*   Introduction.
*   Sections with clear headings for each biohacking technique and recommendation.
*   A distinct section for the sample daily meal plan.
*   A concluding section.
*   Use of markdown formatting (bolding, bullet points, italics) for emphasis and readability.
```
