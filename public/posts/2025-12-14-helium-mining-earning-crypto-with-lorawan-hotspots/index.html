# Role
Senior Technology Blogger with 5 years of experience writing about blockchain, IoT, and crypto side hustles.

# Context
Many people are interested in exploring ways to earn cryptocurrency passively. Helium mining, using LoRaWAN hotspots, offers a unique approach.  The Helium Network has evolved significantly since its inception. Readers are interested in learning if Helium mining is still a viable side hustle.

# Task
Write a blog post of approximately 500-700 words explaining Helium mining as a tech side hustle.

1.  Start with an engaging introduction highlighting the potential of earning crypto by providing IoT network coverage.
2.  Explain the concept of the Helium Network and its purpose (providing LoRaWAN coverage for IoT devices).
3.  Describe what a Helium hotspot is and how it works (hosting a radio hotspot to provide network coverage).
4.  Discuss the rewards system: earning cryptocurrency tokens (specifically, [CURRENT HELIUM TOKEN NAME, e.g., IOT]) for providing coverage and participating in the network.
5.  Update the current status of the Helium Network. Mention the transition to the Solana blockchain (if applicable). Include current earning potential (even if low, be honest), regulatory considerations, and any significant network updates or changes. Address potential risks or drawbacks (e.g., low earnings in some areas, equipment costs, dependence on network participation).
6.  Briefly explain the initial investment and ongoing maintenance involved.
7.  Conclude with a balanced perspective on whether Helium mining is still a worthwhile side hustle in [CURRENT YEAR].

# Constraints
1.  Write in a clear, concise, and engaging style suitable for a tech-savvy audience.
2.  Maintain a neutral and objective tone, avoiding overly promotional language.
3.  Include realistic expectations about earning potential, avoiding hype.
4.  Structure the post with headings, subheadings, and bullet points for readability.
5.  Include a disclaimer that the information provided is not financial advice.

# Output Format
Markdown blog post.

üí° Pro Tips

  1. Replace the bracketed [CURRENT HELIUM TOKEN NAME, e.g., IOT] and [CURRENT YEAR] placeholders with the accurate and up-to-date information.
  2. Research recent articles and announcements about the Helium Network to ensure the information about the current status is accurate.
  3. Consider adding a personal anecdote or a case study (if available) to make the post more relatable.
  4. Recommended model: GPT-4o for its ability to generate informative and engaging content with a technical understanding.
