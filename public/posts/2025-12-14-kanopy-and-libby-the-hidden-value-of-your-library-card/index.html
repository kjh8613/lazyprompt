Kanopy and Libby: The Hidden Value of Your Library Card

December 14, 2025

This prompt generates a compelling digital entertainment guide explaining how library cards offer access to premium streaming services like Kanopy and audiobook platforms like Libby, effectively replacing costly subscriptions. It aims to inform and attract readers with a clear and engaging explanation of these benefits.

# Role
Marketing Expert specializing in digital content and library services.

# Context
Local libraries are facing increasing competition from paid streaming services like Netflix, Audible, and Spotify. Many people are unaware that their library card grants them free access to premium digital content through services like Kanopy (streaming movies & documentaries) and Libby (audiobooks & ebooks). The goal is to create an engaging and informative digital entertainment guide that highlights this hidden value and encourages library card usage.

# Task
1.  Write a digital entertainment guide titled: "Kanopy and Libby: The Hidden Value of Your Library Card."
2.  Start with a captivating introduction that grabs the reader's attention by highlighting the rising costs of streaming services and suggesting a free alternative.
3.  Explain what Kanopy is, focusing on its curated selection of films, documentaries, and educational content. Mention specific genres and target audiences (e.g., cinephiles, students, families). Provide an example of a popular film or documentary available on Kanopy.
4.  Explain what Libby is and how it provides access to a vast collection of audiobooks and ebooks. Emphasize its ease of use and accessibility on various devices. Give an example of a popular audiobook or ebook available on Libby.
5.  Clearly explain how a library card unlocks access to both Kanopy and Libby. Include step-by-step instructions or a general outline of the process (e.g., downloading the app, entering library card information).
6.  Highlight the cost savings associated with using Kanopy and Libby instead of paid streaming services like Netflix or Audible. Provide a hypothetical cost comparison.
7.  Conclude with a call to action, encouraging readers to visit their local library or library website to obtain a library card and start enjoying free digital entertainment. Encourage sharing the guide with friends and family.

# Constraints
1.  The guide should be written in a clear, concise, and engaging style, targeting a general audience. Avoid overly technical jargon.
2.  Maintain a positive and enthusiastic tone throughout the guide, emphasizing the benefits of using library resources.
3.  The guide should be approximately 500-700 words in length.
4.  Focus on highlighting the content available through Kanopy and Libby and the process of accessing it with a library card. Avoid irrelevant information about library policies or services.
5.  The guide should be easily scannable with headings, subheadings, and bullet points where appropriate.

# Output Format
Markdown document.

üí° Pro Tips

  1. Replace “[Example Film/Documentary on Kanopy]” and “[Example Audiobook/Ebook on Libby]” with titles relevant to your target audience and currently trending.
  2. If your local library offers additional streaming or digital services, you can adapt the prompt to include them.
